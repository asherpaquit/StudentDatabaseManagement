Admission Process

We are excited that you're considering joining CIT-U! Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and student-friendly. Follow the steps below to apply:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (e.g., transcripts, ID, test scores).
  3. Attend an interview with our admissions team.
  4. Await your admission decision via email.

Application Requirements

To apply, please ensure you have the following documents ready:

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of high school transcript
  • Government-issued ID
  • Two passport-sized photos
