Our Mission

At CIT-U, our mission is to provide high-quality education that
nurtures the intellectual, technical, and social skills of our
students. We strive to create a learning environment that encourages
critical thinking, creativity, and a commitment to lifelong learning.
Through our comprehensive programs and dedicated faculty, we aim to prepare
our students for the challenges and opportunities of the modern world.

Vision

Our vision is to be a beacon of excellence in education,
innovation, and societal development. We aspire to be
recognized globally as a leading institution that not only
imparts knowledge but also fosters a culture of critical
thinking, creativity, and ethical leadership.

Core Values

At Cebu Institute of Technology - University (CIT-U), our core values are the guiding principles that define our culture, shape our decisions, and inspire our community. These values are integral to our mission of providing an exceptional education and fostering a supportive and dynamic learning environment.

Excellence
We strive for the highest standards in all we do. Our commitment to excellence drives us to pursue academic rigor, innovative research, and continuous improvement. We encourage our students and faculty to reach their full potential and achieve outstanding results.

Integrity
We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our actions. Integrity is the foundation of our relationships and interactions. We promote honesty, transparency, and accountability within our community, ensuring that our actions are guided by fairness and respect.

Innovation
We foster a culture of creativity and forward-thinking. Innovation is at the heart of our approach to education and research. We encourage our students and faculty to challenge conventional thinking, explore new ideas, and develop solutions that address real-world problems.
