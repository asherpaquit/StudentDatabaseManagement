Our Mission

At CIT-U, our mission is to provide high-quality education that nurtures the intellectual, technical, and social skills of our students. We strive to create a learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and a commitment to lifelong learning. Through our comprehensive programs and dedicated faculty, we aim to prepare our students for the challenges and opportunities of the modern world.

Vision

Our vision is to be a beacon of excellence in education, innovation, and societal development. We aspire to be recognized globally as a leading institution that not only imparts knowledge but also fosters a culture of critical thinking, creativity, and ethical leadership.

Core Values

At Cebu Institute of Technology - University (CIT-U), our core values are the guiding principles that define our culture, shape our decisions, and inspire our community. These values are integral to our mission of providing an exceptional education and fostering a supportive and dynamic learning environment.

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all we do. Our commitment to excellence drives us to pursue academic rigor, innovative research, and continuous improvement.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our actions, promoting honesty, transparency, and accountability within our community.
  • Innovation: We foster a culture of creativity and forward-thinking, encouraging our students and faculty to challenge conventional thinking and explore new ideas.
