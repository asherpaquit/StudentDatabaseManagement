Our Mission

At CIT-U, our mission is to provide high-quality education that
nurtures the intellectual, technical, and social skills of our
students. We strive to create a learning environment that encourages
critical thinking, creativity, and a commitment to lifelong learning.
Through our comprehensive programs and dedicated faculty, we aim to prepare
our students for the challenges and opportunities of the modern world

About CIT

Welcome to Cebu Institute of Technology - University (CIT-U), a premier educational
institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development.
Established in 1946, CIT-U has a rich history of producing globally competitive
graduates who excel in various fields.
