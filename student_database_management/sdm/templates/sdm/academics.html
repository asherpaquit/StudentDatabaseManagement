Academics


Elementary

Our elementary program provides a strong foundation for young learners, emphasizing basic academic subjects such as reading, mathematics, science, and social studies. Students are encouraged to explore their curiosity, develop creativity, and foster a love for learning through hands-on activities and interactive lessons. Our goal is to nurture their growth in a safe and supportive environment, preparing them for future academic challenges.

Senior and Junior High School

Our Senior and Junior High School programs provide students with a rigorous academic curriculum that prepares them for higher education and professional life. We offer specialized tracks, including STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), Humanities, and Business, allowing students to focus on their areas of interest. With a blend of academic knowledge, skill development, and extracurricular opportunities, our programs are designed to produce well-rounded individuals ready for college and beyond.

College

Our college program offers a diverse range of majors and degrees designed to prepare students for successful careers in their chosen fields. With access to advanced courses, experienced faculty, and hands-on research opportunities, students can build expertise in their areas of interest. Our college curriculum focuses on both academic excellence and practical experience, empowering students to excel in an increasingly competitive global workforce.

Welcome to Cebu Institute of Technology - University (CIT-U), a premier educational institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development. Established in 1946, CIT-U has a rich history of producing globally competitive graduates who excel in various fields.

Life at CIT-U is vibrant and dynamic. Our campus is home to a diverse student body and offers a range of extracurricular activities, from student organizations and sports teams to cultural events and community service projects. These activities provide students with opportunities to develop leadership skills, build lasting friendships, and engage with the broader community.
